# My Notes

## Getting Started with MindVault

Welcome to MindVault! This is your personal knowledge base.

Add your notes here in any format. MindVault will:
- Parse sections (`##`) into browsable items
- Generate AI summaries via the Wiki feature
- Categorize your content automatically

## How to Add Content

### Quick Notes
- Drop quick ideas and thoughts here as bullet points
- They appear in the Overview dashboard

### Articles & References
- Place `.md` files in `data/articles/` for long-form reading material
- Place references in `data/saves/` for saved links and summaries
- Place conversation exports in `data/conversations/`

## Tips
- Use `##` headings to organize sections
- Each bullet item is counted in your stats
- The Wiki feature works best when documents have clear structure
